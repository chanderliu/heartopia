## Quick Reference

| Crop | Season | Plant Day | Harvest Day | Re-harvestable |
|------|--------|-----------|-------------|----------------|
| Strawberry | Spring | Day 1 | Day 3 | Yes |
| Cabbage | Spring | Day 1 | Day 4 | No |
| Watermelon | Summer | Day 1 | Day 5 | No |
| Tomato | Summer | Day 1 | Day 3 | Yes |
| Pumpkin | Fall | Day 1 | Day 6 | No |

## Planning Tips

- Plant re-harvestable crops (Strawberries, Tomatoes) early in the season for maximum yield
- Save high-profit single-harvest crops (Pumpkins, Watermelons) for mid-season
- Use the last 2-3 days of a season for quick crops that will finish before the season ends

::: tip Seasonal Planning
Spring: Prioritize Strawberries for repeat harvests. Summer: Mix Tomatoes (re-harvest) with Watermelons. Fall: Pumpkins are the most profitable single crop.
:::
